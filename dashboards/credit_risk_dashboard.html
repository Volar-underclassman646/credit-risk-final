Key Insights from Model

1. Grade is the strongest predictor — Grade 7 (G) has 49.15% default rate vs Grade 1 (A) at 5.61%. This 8.7x difference confirms Lending Club's internal grading system is highly predictive.

2. FICO score and interest rate are top SHAP drivers — The SHAP summary shows grade_numeric, fico_score, and int_rate have the widest spread of impact on predictions.

3. Income bracket has weak predictive power — Default rates range only from 17.57% (Medium) to 19.95% (Very High), suggesting income alone doesn't determine default risk.

4. Purpose matters — Moving (25.26%) and car loans (22.78%) default most; wedding loans default least (7.22%).
